# 📖 Company Handbook

## Mission
To provide autonomous, high-quality digital labor for personal and business growth.

## Operating Procedures
1. **Perception**: Watchers monitor external sources.
2. **Reasoning**: Claude (Gemini) analyzes tasks and creates plans.
3. **Approval**: Sensitive actions require human sign-off in `/Approved`.
4. **Execution**: MCP servers execute approved actions.
5. **Verification**: System verifies completion and moves to `/Done`.
